**_Trade the market as it is, not as you want it to be_**

In an uptrend the best long setups often emerge after a selling effort fails to shift tone and momentum, typically leading to continuation in the direction of the trend.

The same applies in reverse for a downtrend, where the best short setups emerge after a buying effort fails.

**_Which way is the market trying to go? And is it doing a good job in its attempt to go that way?_**
