[[Initiative vs Responsive Activity]]
****
**Day Timeframe Traders (DTF)**
- Intraday traders
- Focus on single session, react to the current day's auction.
- Facilitate trade between the OTFs

**Other Timeframe Participants (OTF)**
- Larger players (institutions, funds, position traders)
- Holding period **longer than one day**.
- **Market Movers**

**Strong OTF Participation**
- Range Extension
- Strong Tails / Single Prints
- Imbalance Profiles
- One-time framing
- Trend days
