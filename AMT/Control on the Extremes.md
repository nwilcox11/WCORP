[[Timeframe Participants]]
****
**Tails**
- Tails are created when an aggressive buyer or seller enters the market on an extreme and quickly moves price.
- Two TPOs long to be considered significant.
- **Tail at the upper extreme**
	- Strong seller entered the market and drove price to lower levels.
- **Tail at the lower extreme**
	- Strong buyer entered the market and drove price to higher levels.

**Range Extension**
- Multiple period range extension is generally the result of successively higher or lower auctions.
- The stronger the control, the more frequent and elongated the range extension.
	- More elongated profile

**TPO Count**
- Sum of all TPOs above and below the POC. (Ignoring single prints)
- Total **above** POC = **Other timeframe sellers willing to sell and stay short above value**.
- Total **below** POC = **Other timeframe buyers willing to buy and stay long below value**.
- This ratio is a indication of **buyer/seller imbalance**.
