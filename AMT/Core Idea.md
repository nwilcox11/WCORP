**Markets can be viewed as a continuous auctions where buyers and sellers negotiate prices.**

**Finished Auction at high** 
- Aggressive selling (asks) exhausted buyers at that level
- **0 bids x some asks** at the high

**Finished Auction at low**
- Aggressive buying (bids) exhausted sellers at that level
- **O asks x some bids** at the low.

**Unfinished Auction at High**
- Both bid and ask volume
- Presence of **Bid Volume** aggressive sellers where still hitting bids even at the absolute high of the move, buyers did not exhaust the selling pressure, upward auction feels "unfinished"

**Unfinished Auction at Low**
- Both bid volume and ask volume
- presence of **Ask Volume** here means aggressive buyers were still lifting offers even a the absolute low. Sellers did not fully exhaust the buying interest.

We can think of these Unfinished auctions as areas of interest to revisit.

Aggressive vs Passive Participants

| Action             | Who is Aggressive? | What They Do      | What Shows in Footprint         | Meaning            |
| ------------------ | ------------------ | ----------------- | ------------------------------- | ------------------ |
| Hit the Bid        | Seller             | Market sell order | Bid volume (usually left side)  | Aggressive selling |
| Lift the Ask/Offer | Buyer              | Market buy order  | Ask volume (usually right side) | Aggressive buying  |
