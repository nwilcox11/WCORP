[[Timeframe Participants]]
****
**Daily one-time framing down (OTFD)**
- Bearish market structure indicating sellers are in control
- **_Each subsequent bar forms a lower high, signaling a strong downward trend._**
	- Lower **highs** bar after bar.
- This pattern is considered over when market makes a **high** that exceeds the previous bar's **high**.
- Generally require 3 bars

**Daily one-time framing up (OTFU)**
- Bullish market structure indicating buyers are in control
- **_Each subsequent bar forms a higher low, signaling a strong upward trend_**
	- Higher **lows** bar after bar.
- This pattern is considered over when market makes a **low** that is **lower** than the previous bar's **low**.
- Generally require 3 bars

**Balance**
- A market condition where price consolidates within a defined range
- Reflects indecision as market awaits more market-generated information.
- **Apply balance guidelines**
	- Fade trades at ranges extremes **(Highs/Lows)**.
	- Prepare for breakout setups if balances resolves.
